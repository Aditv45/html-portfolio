The best movies according to me

Top three movies for me:

"Intersteller"

A very inspiring and intelligently crafted movie about time travel, space, blackholes .
Must watch for science lovers.

"Sam Bahadur"

A great story about a brave hero told and potrayed flawlessly.
Intense acting and superb dialogues.

"Shutter island"

Full of mind blowing twist and suspense.
The ending will leave you stupefied.
